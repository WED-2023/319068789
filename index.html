History

I was born in 1947 in Morocco in Ezer Faiz.
We had a very good time in Morocco, we had everything in abundance and we were very rich.
I grew up in Morocco until the age of 6 and in 1953 immigrated to Israel.
In Israel we lived 9 people in a small and crowded house.
I attended school for 8 years, after which I went to work in the carpentry trade until I enlisted in the army.

Hobbies

When I was a little boy I loved to grow plants.
As a child I really liked taking care of dogs.
Bodybuilding - I trained for 4 years, of which I coached for 2 years.
During the training period there were competitions in which I participated and won first place in "Mr. Hanegev" and participated in "Mr. Israel".
I really like football.
